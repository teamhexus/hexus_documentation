1. Click on **Settings** on the left-side panel.
2. Navigate to the **Customization** section.
3. Scroll down to find the Pronunciation**Dictionary**  and click **Add Pronunciation**.
4. Enter your desired **word or phrase** in the text field.
5. Click the **Listen** button to hear how it sounds.
6. Click **Save** to store your changes.

Your custom pronunciation is now active and will automatically apply to AI voiceovers and AI avatar videos.
