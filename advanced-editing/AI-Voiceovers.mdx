Adding an AI voiceover is super simple following these steps:

1. Select the video you want to add voiceovers to and click the **Edit** button.
2. On the right panel, go to the **Speech & Video** option.
3. Modify the text if needed to match what you want the voiceover to say.
4. Choose the most suitable voice from the list to fit your video's tone.
5. Click on the sound button to preview how the voice sounds.
6. Confirm your choice once youâ€™ve found the perfect voice.
7. Click **Generate Speech** to create an audio version of your text.
8. Toggle the switch to **on** if you want speech generation on all screens of your video.
9. Hit **Publish** to finalize your video with your new AI voiceover!

<Tip>
  **Pro Tips:**

  - Keep the speaker and voice settings (speed, stability & similarity) same across all steps for consistent voiceovers.
  - Punctuations around beginning and ending of a sentence influence the voice for the whole sentence. Minimize the usage for a consistent voice across all steps.
  - Perfect the pronunciations of specific words like business terms by using our [pronunciation dictionary](https://support.hexus.ai/advanced-editing/pronunciation-dictionary) (English only)
</Tip>
