### **Getting started without the Chrome Extension**

To create a flow without recording your screen:

1. Go to [**app.usehexus.com/scratch**](https://app.usehexus.com/scratch)
2. On your dashboard, click on **‚ÄúCreate from scratch‚Äù**

   ![Image(98) Pn](/images/image\(98\).png)
3. Choose your content source:
   - **Upload a video** (MP4 format recommended)
   - **Upload images** (JPG, PNG, or GIF)
   - **Import from Loom** by pasting the Loom video URL

   ![Image(95) Pn](/images/image\(95\).png)

- After uploading your desired files, add a script for your AI voiceover, AI Avatar or record your own video and audio

  ![Image(96) Pn](/images/image\(96\).png)
  - Click on Done  to finish building your flow\!

### **Why use this method?**

- **No Chrome Extension required**: Great for users on restricted browsers or corporate devices.
- **Repurpose existing content**: Turn marketing videos, support walkthroughs, or product clips into reusable interactive demos.

---

### **Best practices**

- Keep your flows short and focused, ideally under 2 minutes for higher engagement.
- Use a **clear video resolution (720p or higher)** to ensure text and UI elements are visible.
- Combine multiple images or clips into one seamless flow for a guided experience.
