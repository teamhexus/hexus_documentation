Creating an interactive video on Hexus is straightforward through its powerful Chrome extension and intuitive dashboard. This guide will walk you through the entire process, from setup to final publishing.

### **Getting Started: Set Up**

1. **Install the Hexus Chrome Extension**
   - Download the Chrome extension using [**this link**](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/hexus/ihickminlacpaeajbecffaoplmjlofao).
   - Pin the extension to your browser toolbar for quick access. This is how the Chrome extension should look like üëá

![image.png](/images/image.png)

Find more details on the Chrome extension [here](https://support.hexus.ai/getting-started/extension).

2. **Record Your First Flow**

- Click on the Hexus extension icon to start recording.
- Capture your screen, clicks, and typing actions. Hexus automatically cleans up the recording to create an interactive product tour with editable captions.

3. **Access the Hexus Dashboard**
   - Once your recording is complete, head to the Hexus dashboard to begin editing.

### **Editing Your Video**

Hexus offers extensive customization options, including branding, typography, and cursor settings for polished designs. You can add voiceovers, professional avatars, and additional clips for media-rich content. Optimize videos with auto zoom, multilingual support, and advanced player settings.

Use precise editing tools for section-specific adjustments and content redaction. Finally, convert videos into different formats and publish with a single click. Find more information on editing your tour [here](https://support.hexus.ai/getting-started/editing-your-tour).

### **Publishing and Sharing Your Video**

- Once satisfied with your edits, click **Publish** to make your changes live.
- Share your polished, professional video with your audience across various channels. More details on publishing your video [here](https://support.hexus.ai/getting-started/publishing-and-sharing).
