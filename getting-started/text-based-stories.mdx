#

**Get Started**

Text-based stories simplify product storytelling to create engaging, interactive content catered to each segment of your audience. 

Text-based stories on Hexus combine written content with interactive elements.&#x20;

**Hexus Tip:** Make the most of the preview feature to experience your story from the user's perspective before publishing. Add notes on brand guidelines, formatting, messaging, and any information. This helps ensure your content flows naturally and delivers the intended impact.

**Creating Your First Story**

* To begin creating, log in to app.usehexus.com and head to ‘Create Text Guide’

* Once on the page, enter your prompt, and any additional context, and choose the format that you want. Remember to be as elaborative as you can, this will help in getting the best result.

* All changes are saved as drafts automatically. Remember to publish your story before sharing it with your audience.

**Text-Based Story Elements and Features**

Hexus offers various features for creating interactive content that resonates with your audience:

1. **Outline generation**: Create quick outlines for multiple formats and edit them easily while keeping your team in the loop

2. **Switch between multiple templates**: Create a step-by-step guide, a product update or a blog as per your customer’s needs

3. **Generate content**: Get the final content designed as per the prompts, additional context and your previous Hexus guides

4. **Custom interactions**: Add specific number selections (cards or order of the outline) or add your brand guidelines to enhance the content

5. **Image uploads**: Add any supporting image that might further add to the story

**Building Your Story**

In a nutshell, all stories that you’ll build will have these elements:

* Select your story type

* Add a detailed prompt and additional context on your brand guidelines

* Add and arrange content elements in sequence

* Customize interactive elements

* Preview, edit, and refine your story

* Publish when ready
