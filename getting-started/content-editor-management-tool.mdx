### **Get Started**

The **Content Editor Management Tool** gives you the ability to define custom **HTML text replacement rules** used by the Hexus Content Editor in the Chrome extension.

1. Click on **Settings** from the left-side panel.
2. Select **Content Editor Management Tool** from the available options.
3. Enter your preferred **Configuration Name**.
4. Add your desired **replacement rules** in the input fields.
5. Click on **Create Configuration** to save your rule set.

Your configuration is now active and will be used by the Hexus Content Editor within the Chrome extension.

### **Frequently Asked Questions**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who has access to the Content Editor Management Tool?">
    Users on the **Starter plan and above** can create and manage configurations. Team sharing is only available on **Growth+ plans**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where are these rules used?">
    The replacement rules you configure are applied inside the **Hexus Content Editor Chrome extension**, ensuring consistent content formatting during editing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I share my configuration with teammates?">
    Yes, if you're on a **Growth+ plan**, you can share configurations with your entire team so everyone can use the same rules.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I want to update a rule after creating it?">
    You can go back into the **Content Editor Management Tool** to edit, update, or delete any existing configurations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
