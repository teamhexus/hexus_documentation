## A list of all the branding assets you can upload include:

1. Image: Add images that can serves as an intro or outro slide, screen background, form background, or watermark.
    a. Cover image
    b. Custom image
    d. Watermark 

<img height="800" width="400" src="/images/image-custom-assets.png" />

2. Documents or Text: Upload documents or paste the information in text format to customize AI generated titles, captions, and script for text-to-speech and body
    a. Product info
    b. Brand guidelines
    c. Customer overview
    d. Glossary

<img height="800" width="400" src="/images/document-custom-assets.png" />
  
3. Fonts: Up to 5 fonts can be uploaded as .ttf or .otf

<img height="800" width="400" src="/images/font-custom-assets.png" />

4. Custom voice: Don't want an AI voice? You can add your own voice or any other custom voice of your choosing to add to your demos and videos.

<img height="400" width="800" src="/images/custom-font-and-voice.png" />

5. Background Audio: If you have a standard background audio that is added to all your brand videos and demos, you can do it easily in Hexus too! Upload up to 3 .mp3 audio files for this purpose. 

<img height="800" width="400" src="/images/audio-custom-assets.png" />

6. Custom Video: Have some product videos already? You can add them here and use them in any new Hexus flows you make.

<img height="800" width="400" src="/images/video-custom-assets.png" />

7. Customize Hexus Public Links: Add your brand identity to the hexus links you share with customers or add to your website.
   a. Add Logos, Watermarks, and Thumbnails
   b. Create Custom Calls-To-action

<img height="400" width="800" src="/images/customizing-with-logo-watermark-thumbnail.png" />
