Want to automatically sync your Hexus leads to [Apollo.io](http://Apollo.io) for powerful enrichment and outreach capabilities? This integration makes it super simple to connect both platforms in just two clicks. Let's walk through the quick setup process that'll have you syncing leads in under a minute.

1. First, you'll need to access your integrations hub where all available connections live. Open your Hexus dashboard and look for the **Integrations** section in your main navigation menu. You can also navigate directly to https://app.usehexus.com/integrations to save a click.
2. On the [**Apollo.io**](http://Apollo.io)** **integrations card, click on the **Connect Now** option - you'll be redirected to Apollo's login and permissions page.
3. If prompted, log in to [**Apollo.io**](http://Apollo.io) with your credentials and grant permissions for **Hexus **by clicking on the **Authorize** button
4. And that's it! You are now ready to sync your [**Hexus Form Leads**](https://support.hexus.ai/advanced-editing/lead-capture-form#adding-a-lead-capture-form) with [Apollo.io](http://Apollo.io) contacts
