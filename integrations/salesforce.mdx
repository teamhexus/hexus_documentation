Follow these simple steps to get started:

1. Click on **Integrations** in the left-side panel.
2. On the Salesforce tile, click the **Connect now** button.
3. On the Salesforce authentication page, click **Allow** to grant Hexus AI permission to sync leads data with your account.

Once complete, Salesforce will be listed as a connected integration and your data will begin syncing.
