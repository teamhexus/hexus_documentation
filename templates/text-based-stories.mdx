**Get Started**

Text-based stories simplify product storytelling to create engaging, interactive content catered to each segment of your audience.

Text-based stories on Hexus combine written content with interactive elements.

**Hexus Tip:** Make the most of the preview feature to experience your story from the user's perspective before publishing. Add notes on brand guidelines, formatting, messaging, and any information. This helps ensure your content flows naturally and delivers the intended impact.

**Creating Your First Story**

 1. **Log in to Hexus** : Go to [app.usehexus.com](http://app.usehexus.com) and log in with your account credentials.
 2. **Navigate to 'Create Text Guide'** : From the dashboard, select the **'Create Text Guide'** option to begin.
 3. **Enter prompt and context** : Input a clear prompt along with any relevant background information to improve content accuracy.
 4. **Select a content format** : Choose the desired format for the story, such as a blog post, product launch update, newsletter, or social media post.
 5. **Add references to existing Hexus flows** : Reference multiple Hexus flows to enrich the story with relevant information from existing guides.
 6. **Create an outline** : Click **'Create Outline'** to create a structured overview of the content. Use the format switcher to preview different layout styles.
 7. **Describe your audience** : Select relevant segments such as Business, Sales Leaders, Marketing, or Customer Success to describe audience.
 8. **Customize the tone** : Select a tone based on the target audience. Available options include:
    - Professional
    - Conversational
    - Data-driven
    - Informative
    - Educational
 9. **Edit the content flow** : Rearrange or modify the content structure as required.
10. **Create the full story** : After finalizing the flow, select **'Create Document'** to create the full draft.
11. **Review and collaborate** : Edit the generated content as needed. Use the commenting feature to collaborate with team members.
12. **Save and publish** : Drafts are saved automatically. To finalize, click **'Publish'** before sharing externally.

### **Text-Based Story Elements and Features**

### Hexus offers various features for creating interactive content that resonates with your audience:

1. **Outline generation**: Create quick outlines for multiple formats and edit them easily while keeping your team in the loop
2. **Switch between multiple templates**: Create a step-by-step guide, a product update or a blog as per your customer's needs
3. **Generate content**: Get the final content designed as per the prompts, additional context and your previous Hexus guides
4. **Reference multiple Hexus flows**: Leverage past stories to maintain consistency and improve relevance.
5. **Customize tone**: Set a unique voice or tone per article, ensuring brand consistency across use cases.
6. **Custom interactions**: Add specific number selections (cards or order of the outline) or add your brand guidelines to enhance the content
7. **Image uploads**: Add any supporting image that might further add to the story
