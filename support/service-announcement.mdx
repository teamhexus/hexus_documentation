## Hexus is joining Harvey AI

We're excited to share that **Hexus is joining Harvey AI**. As part of this transition, Hexus services will continue to be provided until **April 20, 2026**, after which we will be shutting down the services.

We want to ensure a smooth transition for all our customers. Below are the key changes and answers to frequently asked questions.

---

## Key Changes

1. **Hexus is no longer taking new sign-ups** - New account registrations have been disabled.
2. **Existing customers can continue to use Hexus, free of charge** - All existing customers can continue using Hexus on their current plans at no cost until April 20, 2026.
3. **All charges have been paused** - Your account and credit cards will not be charged for services. We will continue to honor your Hexus subscription and features free of charge until April 20, 2026 or the date of cancellation, whichever is earlier.

---

## Next Steps

We recommend that you:

1. **Continue using Hexus** - Your services will remain fully functional until April 20, 2026
2. **Plan your migration** - Evaluate alternative platforms like Arcade or Storylane for your product demo needs
3. **Export your data** - Ensure you export all your flows, videos, and content before the shutdown date
4. **Reach out with questions** - Contact us at team@hexus.ai or via in-app Intercom chat if you need assistance

Thank you for being part of the Hexus community. We appreciate your support and are here to help make this transition as smooth as possible.
